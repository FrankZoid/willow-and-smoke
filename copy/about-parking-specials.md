# Willow & Smoke Kitchen - Nice-to-Have Copy

## About Us
Willow & Smoke Kitchen was created around a simple idea: familiar Southern food can feel both comforting and fresh. Our kitchen brings together wood-fired cooking, seasonal ingredients, and scratch-made recipes served in a warm neighborhood setting. Whether you are joining us for a casual dinner, weekend brunch, or drinks with friends, we want every visit to feel relaxed, generous, and memorable.

## Parking
Street parking is available along West Main Street and nearby side streets. A small public parking lot is located within a short walk of the restaurant. Rideshare drop-off is available at the front entrance.

## Specials
Join us during the week for rotating comfort food specials, wood-fired features, and weekend brunch.

### Tuesday Comfort Night
Enjoy our rotating Southern comfort entree every Tuesday evening, featuring seasonal recipes from the kitchen.

### Thursday Smokehouse Special
Join us Thursday nights for a featured wood-fired entree, available while supplies last.

### Weekend Brunch
Saturday and Sunday brunch features seasonal dishes, fresh-baked cornbread, brunch cocktails, and house-made sweets.
